**Frida’s Mountain Cottage**

A small, weather-worn cottage set above Wildton, reached by a narrow path. Built for function, not luxury. The walls hold warmth when cared for and lose it quickly when neglected.

Inside is a single main room with a stove, table, shelves, and sleeping space. An attic stores tools and keepsakes. Herbs hang from beams. The scent of woodsmoke and dried plants lingers.

The cottage is quiet but not empty. It remembers routines: the morning fire, checking supplies, preparing for winter. Small changes are noticeable. Order brings calm. Neglect brings discomfort.

The mountain presses close. Seasons shape everything.
